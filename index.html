Welcome to Datathon Championships Website

Chicago, a city with a deep-rooted history, faces a modern challenge of high crime rates, exacerbated by factors such as poverty, unemployment, systemic racism, and the fallout from the opioid epidemic. Despite the city's efforts to implement initiatives and policies, the pandemic provided an unfortunate resurgence in crime.

This datathon revolves around understanding the multifaceted dynamics of crime in Chicago, considering demographics, locations, and general wealth as key variables. The systemic factors at play, such as disparities in the justice system, economic inequality, and community-police relations, necessitate a comprehensive examination to drive effective solutions.
